Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the K-12 Smart Attendance System collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information. By using the system, you agree to the terms outlined here.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identification (Name, Student Number, Grade & Section)
  • Contact Information (Email, Phone Number)
  • Attendance Records (time-in, time-out, date, session)
  • System-generated identifiers (aztec code, QR code, or RFID code)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To track and record student attendance accurately
  • To notify parents or guardians of attendance status (if enabled)
  • To assist teachers and administrators in class monitoring
  • To generate reports for academic and administrative purposes

4. Data Storage & Protection

All data is securely stored and accessible only to authorized school personnel. We implement appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your information with third parties. Data may only be shared with:

  • School administrators
  • Authorized teachers
  • Parents or guardians (for notification features)

6. Student & User Rights

  • You may request access to your personal records
  • You may request correction of inaccurate information
  • You may raise concerns regarding data privacy with the school

7. Cookies & Tracking

The system may use basic tracking (session tokens, login state) to maintain user sessions. No advertising or third-party tracking cookies are used.

8. Changes to This Policy

The school reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the system means you accept these updates.

9. Contact Information

For inquiries regarding your data, please contact the school administrator or IT department.
